# Justin Welsh: Distribution

## Tactical Summary
Justin Welsh's lesson is that authority compounds through consistent distribution. Content should create familiarity, sharpen positioning, and make outbound warmer before a rep ever reaches out.

## 3 Actionable Points
1. Publish around one repeatable point of view. Consistency beats variety when the goal is category authority and recall.
2. Repurpose one idea across formats. Turn the same core insight into posts, comments, emails, and lead magnets so distribution does more work.
3. Treat content as demand support. Use audience growth and trust-building to improve response rates, referrals, and inbound conversations downstream.
