# Lead Gen Jay: AI Stack

## Tactical Summary
Lead Gen Jay represents the AI-native outbound operator: compress repetitive work with tools, keep humans focused on judgment, and use AI where it compounds speed without killing relevance.

## 3 Actionable Points
1. Use AI to remove manual research bottlenecks. Let tools summarize accounts, draft angle options, and prepare first-pass personalization for review.
2. Keep humans on final positioning. Use AI for acceleration, but make offer choice, pain-point framing, and campaign approval human-owned.
3. Build a connected stack. Link sourcing, enrichment, writing, QA, and sending tools so prospects move through one operating system instead of scattered one-off prompts.
