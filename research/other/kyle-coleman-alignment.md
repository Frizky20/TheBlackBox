# Kyle Coleman: Sales and Marketing Alignment

## Tactical Summary
Kyle Coleman's operating logic is that pipeline scales better when demand creation and demand capture are aligned around the same ICP, message, and revenue targets.

## 3 Actionable Points
1. Align on one definition of a good opportunity. Sales and marketing should share ICP rules, qualification criteria, and pipeline stage expectations.
2. Mirror messaging across channels. The promise used in ads, content, outbound, and sales calls should feel like one story rather than separate campaigns.
3. Review pipeline quality together. Use regular cross-functional feedback loops so marketing sees conversion quality and sales sees which themes are creating real demand.
