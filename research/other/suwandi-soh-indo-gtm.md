# Suwandi Soh: Indonesia GTM

## Tactical Summary
Suwandi Soh's value is in understanding how B2B SaaS wins in Indonesia: segment the market by maturity, lead with operational ROI, and build trust through local relevance.

## 3 Actionable Points
