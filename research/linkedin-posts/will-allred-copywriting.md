# Will Allred: Copywriting

## Tactical Summary
Will Allred's angle is that reply rate improves when emails are easier to process. Strong copy is clear, concise, and written for the reader's limited attention.

## 3 Actionable Points
1. Cut cognitive load. Use short sentences, plain wording, and one clear idea per message.
2. Focus on the reader's problem. Replace company-centric claims with language that reflects the prospect's likely priorities and friction.
3. Tighten the CTA. End with one low-friction question or next step so the recipient knows exactly how to respond.
