# Guillaume Moubeche: Outreach Psychology

## Tactical Summary
Guillaume Moubeche's strength is lowering resistance in the first touch. The goal is not to force a sale immediately, but to earn enough trust for the conversation to continue.

## 3 Actionable Points
1. Reduce threat in the opener. Use personalized context and soft language so the message feels useful, not extractive.
2. Sell the next step, not the full product. Ask for permission to share an idea or insight instead of pushing directly for a demo.
3. Make relevance obvious fast. Tie the outreach to a specific moment, pain, or observation so the recipient sees why the message is for them.
