# Jordan Crawford: Intent Signals

## Tactical Summary
Jordan Crawford's approach is to contact prospects when there is evidence of motion. Intent and trigger events make outreach more timely, more credible, and easier to convert.

## 3 Actionable Points
1. Prioritize trigger-based outreach. Build lists around funding, hiring, product launches, tech changes, or market expansion instead of static account lists.
2. Translate intent into a business hypothesis. Every signal should connect to a likely pain or initiative, not just appear as a novelty line in the email.
3. Refresh trigger data continuously. Intent decays fast, so run regular enrichment updates and time outreach while the signal still matters.
