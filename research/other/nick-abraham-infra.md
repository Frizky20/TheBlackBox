# Nick Abraham: Cold Email Infrastructure

## Tactical Summary
Nick Abraham's core lesson is that outbound performance depends on durable infrastructure: healthy domains, conservative ramp-up, and fast replacement when reputation slips.

## 3 Actionable Points
1. Maintain redundant sending capacity. Keep extra domains and inboxes warming so campaigns can rotate without losing momentum.
2. Scale volume only when health is stable. Increase sends gradually based on bounce, spam, and engagement signals rather than top-down quota pressure.
3. Protect domain reputation operationally. Use consistent login behavior, warm assets before launch, and replace degraded infrastructure quickly instead of trying to force recovery.
